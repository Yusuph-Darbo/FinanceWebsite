FAQ

How do I create a FinanceFlow account?

You can create an account by downloading our app or visiting our website. Simply enter your basic details, verify your email, and follow the onboarding steps to get started securely.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes — your data is protected with end-to-end encryption, secure cloud storage, and multi-factor authentication. We never share your information with third parties without your consent.

How long do withdrawals take?

Withdrawals are typically processed within 1–2 business days, depending on your bank. Crypto transfers are processed instantly once network confirmations are complete.

What fees should I expect?

FinanceFlow keeps fees transparent. Trading fees start at 0.1%, while deposits are free. Withdrawal fees depend on your payment method and asset type.

Can I use FinanceFlow internationally?

Yes, FinanceFlow is available in most regions worldwide. Certain features may vary depending on local regulations, but core trading and analytics tools are accessible globally.
